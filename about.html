Our Story

Founded in 2005, Mongolian Grill began with a simple idea: that a meal could be more than just food—it could be an experience. We wanted to create a place where every guest could become a chef, crafting a meal that was a perfect reflection of their own taste.

Inspired by the communal tradition of Mongolian barbecue, we've built our restaurant around an open grill and a vibrant selection of the freshest ingredients available. From sourcing high-quality meats and vegetables to mixing our own signature sauces, every detail is chosen with care.

We are a gathering place for friends and family, a spot for a quick and healthy lunch, and a destination for a delicious dinner. Thank you for being a part of our story!
