A Meal Crafted by You.

Founded in 2005, Mongolian Grill began with a simple idea: that a meal could be an experience. We created a place where every guest becomes a chef, crafting a meal that's a perfect reflection of their taste.

Inspired by the communal tradition of Mongolian barbecue, our restaurant is built around an open grill and a vibrant selection of the freshest ingredients available.

Create Your Masterpiece

A Simple, Flavorful Journey

1. Choose Your Ingredients

Fill your bowl with your favorite selection of fresh-cut vegetables, high-quality proteins, and delicious noodles.

2. Create Your Sauce

Mix and match from our array of signature sauces and oils. From sweet teriyaki to fiery chili, craft your unique flavor profile.

3. Watch it Sizzle

Hand your creation to our grill masters. Watch as they cook your meal to perfection on our large, searing-hot grill.
